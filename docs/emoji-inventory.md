# Emoji Inventory

This inventory is based on emoji packs in `src/icons.ts`.
Each pack contains **unique emoji only** (no duplicates within a pack), with
at least **25 icons** per pack.

## Packs Overview

1. **Space & Astronomy** (`space-astronomy`)
2. **Biosphere** (`plants-nature`)
3. **Food & Drinks** (`food-drinks`)
4. **Industry** (`industry`)
5. **World Flags** (`world-flags`)
6. **Science & Tech** (`science-tech`)
7. **Arts & Crafts** (`music-performance`)
8. **Religious Symbols** (`religious-symbols`)

## Similarity Hotspots by Pack

- **Space & Astronomy:** stars and bright sky glyphs can look alike in motion.
- **Biosphere:** flora and small-creature glyphs can blend at small sizes.
- **Food & Drinks:** round fruit and dessert glyphs can be easy to confuse.
- **Industry:** vehicle and tool symbols can share boxy silhouettes.
- **World Flags:** color similarity makes nearby flags harder to distinguish.
- **Science & Tech:** many tool and device symbols share geometric shapes.
- **Arts & Crafts:** instruments and stage icons can look similar in pairs.
- **Religious Symbols:** symbol-heavy glyphs can look alike at quick glance.

## Tuning Notes

- Keep each pack at 25+ unique icons for hard mode safety.
- Keep the total pack count even to preserve the 2-column Settings layout.
- Prefer strong silhouettes and color contrast for quick recognition.
- Re-test with `Debug -> Tiles` after any pack edits.
